LIGHT CHOCOLATE CAKE

Category: Dessert

Ingredients

  • 1/4 pound butter
  • 2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa
  • 3 whole eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon soda
  • 2 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 box of powdered sugar for filling
  • 2 tablespoon butter for filling
  • 1/2 cup cocoa for filling
  • Hot coffee for filling

Directions

  1. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy, add eggs, mix well, then add all dry ingredients, alternating with the milk
  2. Bake in two layers
  3. For filling, melt butter and add powdered sugar, and cocoa for filling
  4. Use enough hot coffee to make soft filling
  5. Spread after cake is cold
